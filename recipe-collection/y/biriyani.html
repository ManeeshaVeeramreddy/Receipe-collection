Chicken Dum Biryani

Chicken Dum Biryani is a rich, aromatic rice dish made with layers of marinated chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and spices. Cooked slowly on dum, this Hyderabadi favorite is full of flavor and tradition.

prep time

45 min

cook time

20 min

serving

6 servings

Tags : Menu

instructions

step 1

Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs. Set aside for at least 30 minutes.

step 2

Cook Rice: Parboil basmati rice with whole spices until 70% cooked.

step 3

Dum Cooking: Layer marinated chicken and rice, seal the pot, and cook on low heat until flavors are infused.

ingredients

2 cups basmati rice

500g chicken

1 cup yogurt

2 onions, sliced

2 tomatoes, chopped

Whole spices (cloves, cardamom, bay leaf)

Salt & chili powder to taste

tools

Heavy-bottomed pot

Spatula

Measuring Cups
