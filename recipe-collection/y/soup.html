Vegetable Soup

Vegetable soup is a warm, nutritious, and comforting dish packed with fresh vegetables and herbs. It’s light yet hearty, making it perfect for any season.

prep time

15 min

cook time

5 min

serving

4 servings

Tags : Menu

instructions

step 1

Sauté onions, garlic, and chopped vegetables in a little oil.

step 2

Add vegetable stock and simmer until vegetables are tender.

step 3

Season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Serve hot.

ingredients

1 cup carrots, chopped

1/2 cup beans, chopped

1/2 cup peas

1 onion, chopped

4 cups vegetable stock

Salt & pepper to taste

tools

Saucepan

Knife

Cutting Board

Measuring Cups
